About VRSC > Membership

Vermont Resident Service Coordinators provides educational and peer support to individuals and organizations offering resident service coordination services in housing across Vermont.

Join today
Complete the VRSC membership form (32kb; PDF) to take advantage of training and education opportunities, gain access to special on-line materials, and network with other professionals who are dedicated to ensuring that resident supportive services are an integral part of Vermont's affordable housing.

Membership fees are used solely to support educational, training, and peer supervision activities for our members.

Benefits of VRSC Membership

  • Two Annual Training and Meetings
  • Certification by VHFA in accordance with HUD guidelines for RSCs
  • Membership List for Networking
